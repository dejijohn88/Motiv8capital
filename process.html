How We Work

Our Process

A structured, predictable path from first conversation to closing — so you always know what happens next and when.

01

Initial Conversation

A direct, no-pressure call with our principals to understand your property, your goals, and your timeline.

02

Confidential Review

We review basic property information under confidentiality — no broad marketing, no exposure to tenants or staff.

03

Underwriting

Our team underwrites the market, the asset, and the numbers with the same rigor used by institutional buyers.

04

Letter of Intent (LOI)

If the opportunity fits our criteria, we issue a clear, straightforward LOI outlining price and terms.

05

Due Diligence

A focused diligence period covering financial, physical, and title review — organized and efficient for both sides.

06

Closing

Reliable execution to closing, with consistent communication so there are no surprises at the table.

07

Asset Management

Post-closing, our operating partners execute the value-creation plan — renovation, leasing, and operations.

What This Means For You
  • A defined timeline instead of an open-ended process
  • Direct principal involvement at every stage
  • Confidentiality protected from first call to closing
  • Clear expectations before you ever sign an LOI
