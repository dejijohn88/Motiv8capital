Property Profile

What we look for in an opportunity

＋

Value-Add Potential

Below-market rents, deferred maintenance, or operational inefficiency that a focused capital and management plan can correct.

＋

Growing Submarkets

Population growth, job creation, and rent growth trends that support a multi-year hold thesis.

＋

Motivated, Direct Sellers

Owners who value certainty of close, discretion, and a straightforward process as much as headline price.

＋

1990s Construction or Newer

Garden-style or mid-rise product with a renovation and repositioning path that pencils conservatively.

＋

Stable-to-Improving Occupancy

Occupancy that supports a value-add underwrite without requiring a full lease-up or turnaround thesis.

＋

Clear Ownership & Title

Straightforward ownership structures that allow due diligence and closing to move on schedule.

Every acquisition is evaluated individually. Criteria above describe our general buy box and are not a binding commitment to purchase any specific property; final terms are subject to underwriting, due diligence, and executed agreements.
