Ross Ramsay

Architecture & Land Planning Advisor

Founder of Ramsay Architecture and Design, a Dallas–Fort Worth architecture and land planning firm he opened in 1963. A registered architect since 1960 and former chairman of the Dallas City Planning Commission, Ross has personally worked on projects totaling over 3.3 million square feet and planned more than 8,000 acres across North Texas. He advises Motiv8 Capital on architecture, land planning, and entitlement strategy as the firm expands into ground-up development.
